About

Who Am I?

I’m Odin, a 4th year computer engineering student at UBC. Throughout my time at UBC, I have taken a variety of courses in software engineering, ranging from data structures and algorithms, to computer communications, to cybersecurity. I have done both front-end (Android, React, and React Native), and back-end (FastAPI, NodeJS, MySQL) development during my co-ops, class projects, and projects with UBC Launchpad.

Interests

I love to read. I enjoy the depth offered by books, both in fiction and non-fiction, that cannot be found in film or television, or in the news. One of my recent favourites is the cult-classic, Battle Royale by Koushun Takami. It is a bit long at 666 pages, but it is an interesting read nonetheless. It isn’t just the Japanese version of the Hunger Games either! As the reader, we get to follow along with many of the students' perspectives throughout the novel. Through this, we get to see the different ways people cope with and act in an impossible situation.

Let me know your book recommendations if you’re a fellow book lover, or even if you just happened to read a great book!
